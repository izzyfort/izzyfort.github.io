Welcome

Welcome to my Health Sciences Portfolio! The purpose of this website is to showcase my academic and professional development as I prepare for a career in healthcare administration, with a focus on becoming a nursing home administrator.

This portfolio features a curated selection of projects that reflect my growth in core health sciences competencies, including health education, health policy, and health promotion and design. Each section highlights how I’ve applied knowledge from the classroom to real-world settings through internships, research, and leadership roles in student organizations. These experiences have deepened my understanding of healthcare systems and operations while strengthening my commitment to compassionate, patient-centered care. As someone preparing to enter the field of long-term care administration, I believe it is essential to demonstrate not only technical knowledge but also empathy, communication, and collaboration. This website provides potential employers and graduate programs with a well-rounded view of my academic strengths, professional readiness, and commitment to enhancing the lives of older adults. You’ll also find my resume and a personal introduction to help you get to know me better, both as an aspiring healthcare leader and as an individual passionate about making a difference.
