About Me

Hello! My name is Izzy Forthaus, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Health Sciences with an emphasis in Leadership and Policy at the University of Missouri. I’m part of the College of Health Sciences and plan to graduate in December 2025. In addition to my major, I am earning a minor in Business and a certificate in Information Systems and Technology to broaden my skills.

I am passionate about improving long-term care and committed to making a positive difference in the lives of older adults. My career goal is to become a nursing home administrator after graduation. Currently, I work as a manager at Andy’s Frozen Custard, where I am developing my leadership and operational skills. I also serve as an intern at Garden View Assisted Living Facility in Valley Park, gaining hands-on experience in senior care, healthcare operations, and facility management. This role has given me valuable insight into the challenges and rewards of healthcare leadership.
